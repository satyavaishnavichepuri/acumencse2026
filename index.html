ABOUT ACUMEN CSE

Acumen CSE is the annual national-level technical symposium of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Vasavi College of Engineering. Since its inception, it has been a platform where innovation meets intellect — drawing the brightest minds to compete, collaborate, and create. Every edition raises the bar with cutting-edge themes, industry-grade challenges, and an electrifying atmosphere.

[THEME LOADING] — the 2026 edition — pushes the frontier further, centering on Quantum Computing and Cybersecurity: two forces reshaping the digital universe. With 16 events spanning technical innovation and creative challenges, this is Acumen's most ambitious edition yet.
